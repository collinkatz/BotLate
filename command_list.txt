-bl help | ?: displays a list of bot commands
-bl config: displays the bot's current configuration
-bl update_config [setting you want to change] [value to change setting to]: changes a bot config setting
-bl join: Joins the bot into your voice channel. Some bot functions can output translated audio to help you learn pronunciation.
-bl leave: Disconnects the bot from your voice channel.
-bl translate [optional: language to translate into] [message to translate]: Translates a message into another language. This function can output translated audio.
-bl list_quizzes: Lists all available quizzes that you can take
-bl quiz [quiz you want to take]: Lets you take a quiz in the language you have indicated in your bot config.
